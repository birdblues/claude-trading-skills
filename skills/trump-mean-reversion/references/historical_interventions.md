## Key Patterns

1. **Verbal interventions precede policy actions** — Watch for escalating rhetoric as a leading indicator
2. **Trade domain has fastest diminishing returns** — Markets learn tariff threat/withdrawal cycle quickly
3. **Energy interventions are most impactful** — Direct executive tools (SPR, OPEC pressure) available
4. **Interest rate interventions are least effective** — Fed independence limits real impact
5. **Cross-domain cascades exist** — Geopolitical de-escalation often triggers energy relief simultaneously
6. **Timing follows news cycle** — Interventions typically occur on weekends or pre-market for maximum headline impact
